§01Summary — about

Lorenz is an ELLIS PhD candidate at Fraunhofer Heinrich Hertz Institute under Prof. Wojciech Samek, with a secondary affiliation at the University of Oxford, where he is co-supervised by Dr. Christian Schröder de Witt and Prof. Philip Torr. His current research is focused on AI for history — building machine-learning systems that help historians read, connect, and reason about historical sources at scale.

Previously he worked on mechanistic interpretability of multimodal foundation models, with a focus on typographic understanding. He co-founded Bliss e.V., a Berlin-based non-profit research collective, and remains a core contributor to the ViT-Prisma toolkit for vision and video interpretability.
